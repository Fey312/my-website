About Us

All Eye Know Beauty is a small brand founded by young women in California who wanted to make lashes more affortable and create styles that no other competitor can offer. We belive that anyone should have the ability to look and feel beautiful without having to worry about affortability. We ensure to make our products to stay at a resonable price for the future to come.

Although we primarly produce lashes, we also create cosmetics such as lip liners and liquid lipsticks. We also carry other products that you would need apply your lashes such as lash glue, lash tweezers, a small electric fan, etc. Us at All Eye Know Beauty, we inspire to offer a expanded range of products from lashes to cosmetics and accessories.
